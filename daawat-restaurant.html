A Message from the Owner

"At Daawat, food is more than sustenance — it is an invitation. An invitation to sit together, to share stories, and to savour the rich tapestry of flavours."

When we opened Daawat Restaurant in the heart of Ulwe, our dream was simple: to bring the true taste of authentic multi-cuisine cooking to our wonderful community — cooked with fresh ingredients, served with love, and enjoyed in an atmosphere that feels like home.

From our signature Biryani and Tandoori Chicken to hearty Dal Makhani, every dish is prepared with recipes perfected over years of passion. Whether you dine in with family, order for a celebration, or grab a quick delivery — we personally ensure every plate leaves our kitchen with the same care and quality.
