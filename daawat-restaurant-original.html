A Message from the Owner

Founder & Host, Daawat Restaurant

"At Daawat, food is more than sustenance — it is an invitation. An invitation to sit together, to share stories, and to savour the rich tapestry of flavours that our incredible cuisine has to offer."

When I opened Daawat Restaurant in the heart of Ulwe, my dream was simple: to bring the true taste of authentic multi-cuisine cooking to our wonderful community — cooked with fresh ingredients, served with love, and enjoyed in an atmosphere that feels like home.

From our signature Biryani and Tandoori Chicken to hearty Dal Makhani, every dish is prepared with recipes perfected over years of passion. Whether you dine in with family, order for a celebration, or grab a quick delivery — I personally ensure that every plate leaves our kitchen with the same care and quality.

Our team is trained not just to serve food, but to create memories. I am grateful to every guest who has made Daawat their favourite restaurant in Ulwe. Come, experience the daawat — the feast — that awaits you!
